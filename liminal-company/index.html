Important Packing Items 

  • Backpack
    You can't bring items if you have nowhere to pack them, so make sure to bring a spacious backpack. We are not viable if any items are lost during the tours.
  • Flashlight
    As seen in the Lone Door thumbnail, these rooms can get pretty dark. Make sure to bring something to light your way, unless you're brave enough to go without one.
  • Water
    Staying hydrated is extremely important, and your guide will bring limited water. Bring a whole days worth of water, as the tours can take longer than expected.
  • Food
    The food your guide brings may not be to your taste, and you may want more. Make sure to pack your own so you can enjoy your breaks.
